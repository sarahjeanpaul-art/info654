The Mission

The JPSA's Commitment

Since its founding in 1946, The Jean Paul School of The Arts has remained committed to fostering independence, creativity, social responsibility, community, and love of the arts. With a combined body of 1,073 students, the faculty and staff of The Jean Paul School of The Arts do not simply seek to educate, but to inspire, encourage, and vitalize our gifted students from around the world through the highest caliber of education and support.
